SUMMARY: Max Temperature

The chart represents max temperature in Farenheit on the y-axis with the latitude on the x-axis. Positive values on the x-axis represent the Northern Hemisphere, negative values represents the Southern Hemisphere, and zero represents the Equator. This polt shows the realationships between temperature and proximty to the equator. As we move closer to zero or the Equator on the x-axis we see an increase in temperature.
